FAQ

Straight answers. No filler. No coping.

What is GridForge?

GridForge is a workflow for generating professional outreach: you provide structured inputs, it drafts clear messaging, and you review + send. It’s built for signal over noise.

What makes GridForge different?

Strong defaults, clean structure, and concise writing. It’s designed to produce messages that feel human and get to the point.

Is it only for email?

No. Email, LinkedIn DMs, follow-ups, and inbound replies are supported. The structure adapts, but the rules stay the same: relevance + clarity + a clear ask.

How do I get better outputs?

Better inputs. Add one specific relevance hook, keep constraints clear, and don’t ask for five things at once. “More detail” is useless. “Keep it under 90 words and ask for 15 minutes” is useful.

Do you store my data?

Treat GridForge like any outbound tool: don’t paste secrets or regulated data. If you operate under strict compliance, keep inputs aligned with your internal policies.

Can I customize tone and templates?

Yes. You can define tone, message type, and objectives. If you want consistent voice across a team, standardize constraints and reuse winning templates.

What’s “message type”?

It tells GridForge how the message should behave: Email is more formal, LinkedIn is shorter, follow-ups assume prior context, inbound replies are responsive and direct.

What should I put in “Additional context”?

Facts that increase relevance: timing, constraints, a reference point, or what the recipient cares about. Avoid fluff. One or two strong details beats a paragraph of vibes.

How does Bulk Generator work?

Upload a CSV where each row is a recipient. GridForge generates one message per row. Then you export results and send via your outbound system.

What headers does Bulk require?

name, company, title, email, notes, industry, goals, messageType

Why do my bulk messages look generic?

Because your rows are generic. Bulk works when “notes” includes a real hook (tool stack, hiring signal, expansion, product launch, KPI pressure).

Can I send directly from GridForge?

You can generate drafts at scale and export them. If you add sending, do it responsibly: verified domains, deliverability basics, and opt-out compliance where required.

Does GridForge guarantee replies?

No. Nothing does. GridForge increases the odds by improving clarity and relevance. Offer + targeting + timing still matter.

What’s the best “first message” structure?

One sentence of relevance, one sentence of value, one clear ask, one easy-out close. If your message needs scrolling, it’s already losing.

Is this “AI writing” detectable?

Not if you keep it short, specific, and human. The giveaway is generic praise, long intros, and over-polished corporate nonsense. Keep it real.
