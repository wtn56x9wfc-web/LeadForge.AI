Frequently Asked Questions

Quick, clear answers — no corporate jargon, no fluff.

What exactly does GridForge do?

GridForge builds high-conversion outreach messages across email, LinkedIn, and SMS. It writes like a real human — not a generic AI robot — so you get more replies and book more calls.

Is the outreach actually personalized?

Yep. You give GridForge the business details, the target, the angle — and it crafts messages tailored to the industry, goals, tone, and channel. Zero copy-paste spam.

Which message types can it generate?

Everything — cold emails, follow-ups, re-engagements, LinkedIn pitches, SMS scripts, intros, call requests, proposals, whatever you need to push pipeline.

Does GridForge replace a sales rep?

Hell no. It replaces the **boring part** — writing. You still run the plays. GridForge just makes you sound sharper and faster than everyone else.

Can I use it for multiple clients?

Yep. Agency owners, SDR managers, and consultants use it to scale messaging across clients without drowning in grunt work.

Is my data stored?

No sensitive data is stored. Your inputs are used to generate the message and that's it.
